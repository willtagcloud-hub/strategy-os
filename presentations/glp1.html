Slide 04 · PWA concept

Medical Weight Eligibility PWA

An education-first PWA that helps users understand options, screens for eligibility, and books consults with licensed clinicians.

Module 1

Goal & readiness quiz

Lifestyle, BMI range, history (no diagnostics).

Module 2

Eligibility screener

Contraindication flags + clinician-friendly handoff.

Module 3

Cost & pathway calculator

Transparent fees and program lengths.

Module 4

Consult booking

Timezone-aware slots, partner clinics.

Module 5

Adherence tools

Reminders + content nudges (where allowed).

Module 6

Refill / continuity prompts

Triggered only with provider approval.

Slide 11 · Compliance

Health-vertical guardrails (white-hat)

  • No "guaranteed weight loss" or specific outcome claims.
  • No before/after body imagery against platform policies.
  • Clinician disclaimers and risk language on every funnel step.
  • Affiliate disclosure on prelander and ad copy where required.
  • HIPAA-safe data handling; minimal PII in tracking.
  • Only licensed providers; pre-approve all ad creative.
  • Avoid personal-attribute callouts ("are you overweight?").
  • Daily disapproval / quality-ranking monitoring.
