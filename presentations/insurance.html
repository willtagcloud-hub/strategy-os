Slide 02 · Market opportunity

Why insurance is the largest lead-gen vertical

  • ~USD 5–7B annual transaction value in performance insurance leads.
  • Speed-to-contact < 1 minute can convert ~4x better than 5-minute response.
  • Exclusive leads command 2–3x the price of shared leads.
  • Medicare AEP (Oct 15 – Dec 7) is the highest-ROI seasonal window in the entire lead-gen industry.

Slide 11 · Compliance

Insurance guardrails

  • State licensing for US lines where applicable; only licensed agencies.
  • TCPA-aligned consent for calls/SMS, store consent logs.
  • HIPAA-safe handling for health-related lines.
  • No "guaranteed lowest rate" or "no questions asked" language.
  • CMS rules for Medicare creative copy.
  • Clear privacy and partner-disclosure pages.
  • Strict per-line ad accounts to avoid policy contamination.
