Slide 07 · Channels

Where to spend

Meta + TikTok (primary)

  • Cheap reach for problem-aware users.
  • Creator content + UGC scales fast.
  • Health policy strict; no body shaming.
  • Creative fatigue is fast.

Native + Google (secondary)

  • Native great for advertorial → quiz.
  • Google Search captures branded + comparison intent.
  • Native quality varies by source.
  • Search CPC variable by ingredient terms.

Slide 11 · Compliance

Supplement-vertical guardrails

  • No drug-like claims; supplements are not treatments.
  • No before/after imagery violating policy.
  • Substantiation behind every benefit claim.
  • Affiliate disclosures and clear refund policy.
  • FTC-aligned testimonial language with results disclaimers.
  • Clear ingredient sourcing and dosage transparency.
  • Brand domain separated from medical-vertical assets.
