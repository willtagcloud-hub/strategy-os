Slide 07 · Channels

Channel split

Google Search (primary)

  • Buyer/mortgage queries are highly local + intent-rich.
  • Geo-bidding precision wins.
  • Premium CPC in major metros.

Meta (secondary)

  • Effective for budget-explorer audiences.
  • Lookalike from buyer-readiness completers scales.
  • Special Ad Category for Housing in US — strict targeting limits.

In the US, Housing falls under Meta's Special Ad Category — plan creatives and audiences accordingly.

Slide 11 · Compliance

Real-estate guardrails

  • Comply with Meta Special Ad Category (Housing) in US — targeting + lookalike rules.
  • Fair Housing Act language; no discriminatory targeting.
  • NMLS rules for any mortgage offers/copy.
  • TCPA-aligned consent for calls/SMS.
  • Local agent licensing verification before partner onboarding.
  • Affiliate disclosure on every page.
  • Use accurate property data sources (no misleading listings).
