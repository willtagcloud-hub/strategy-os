  • SoFi, eToro, Fundrise and similar networks regularly pay USD 100–500 for funded events.
  • Debt consolidation calls can pay USD 25–125+ per qualified lead.

Slide 07 · Channels

Google vs Meta for loan intent

Google Search (primary)

  • Highest intent and conversion.
  • Clear keyword-level ROI signal.
  • Premium CPC on top finance terms.
  • Strict landing-page policy.

Meta (secondary)

  • Cheap top-funnel reach for budgeting/affordability angles.
  • Lead form CTAs lower friction.
  • Personal-attribute policy is strict.
  • Lower intent, higher fraud risk.

Slide 11 · Compliance

Finance-vertical guardrails

  • APR transparency on prelander and result page.
  • No "guaranteed approval" or "no credit check" misleading copy.
  • Licensed-lender claims only; no fake comparisons.
  • Clear T&Cs, privacy, and TCPA-aligned consent for calls/SMS.
  • State licensing checks for US (NMLS where applicable).
  • Affiliate disclosure on every page.
  • Strict separation from gambling/health funnels.
